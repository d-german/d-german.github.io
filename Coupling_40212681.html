Hyland Clinician Window : Coupling

Created by Damon German, last modified on Sep 12, 2022

Coupling is the degree of interdependence between modules or the strength of the relationship between modules.

Software modules should aim for loose coupling.

This is a foundational design principle. Classes that follow the OCP and the DIP are loosely coupled.


See Cohesion, Modularity, Module, Open Closed Principle (OCP), Dependency Inversion Principle (DIP)
